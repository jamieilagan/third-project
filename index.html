Eskinita Art Farm

Is an artist-run project which aims to bring art and culture to the public through gallery exhibitions and community art projects.
It has 5 galleries located within a private residential and farm property in barangay Janopol in Tanauan, Batangas.

Eskinita

noun
Esquinita, from the Spanish word esquina, means “corner” leading to an alleyway. Cities in Metro Manila have networks of side streets and alleyways—eskinitas—familiar streets that take one home, or to yet to be discovered shorter routes to a new adventure, pre-determined destination or a life changing destiny. Eskinita Art Gallery's name is play on founder Alfredo Esquillo's nickname—“Eski.” The Gallery is an “alleyway” to his new home for art and artists, and an invitation to share in his new adventures. As he established his brand of magic cum “social realist” style, his canvases became necessary fixtures in exhibitions that defined junctures in contemporary Philippine Art.

Recent Exhibit

Bayluhanay Biennale 2025:
Sikulo at Sikleta

September 19 to October 19, 2025 (Luzon)
Bayluhanay Biennale invites you into the world of “Sikulo at Sikleta” (Cycle and Cycling) — a theme that asks all of us to pause, look around, and notice the repeating rhythms of nature and the lasting marks our actions leave on the environment. It's a reminder that everything moves in cycles, and that every choice carries an impact.

This year, artists from Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao come together to respond to this call. They share a common mission: to care for our planet and to show how something as simple and powerful as cycling can spark change. As a moving collective, these artist-cyclists turn the streets into their gallery. Their artworks aren't framed on walls, they're worn, carried, and brought directly to the public.

As they ride, art and life weave together, transforming into a live invitation for everyone to join the conversation. Feel the movement, see the message, and be part of this growing community pushing for awareness, action, and a more sustainable future.
